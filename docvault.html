Help & Guide

📤
How to Upload
Click "+ Upload" button or drag & drop any file onto the home screen. Supports PDF, JPG, PNG, DOCX and more.
👁️
View Files In-App
Tap the 👁️ View button on any document. Images open with zoom & rotate controls. PDFs open in a full built-in reader. Use Esc key or the ✕ button to close.
🔍
Zoom & Rotate
While viewing an image, use the ＋ / － buttons to zoom in and out. Use ↻ to rotate. On mobile, pinch with two fingers to zoom.
🇮🇳
DigiLocker Documents
Go to DigiLocker tab → "Add DigiDoc". Fill type, reference, and issuer. Optionally attach a scan. Tap View to see the document card.
⬇️
Download Files
While a file is open in the viewer, click the "⬇ Download" button at the top to save it to your device.
💾
Data Storage
All data including file contents is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. Your files are completely private.
